That is me and my mother. We had a difficult time when we chose to leave our home country
and start a new life in a different country. For a long time, I did not understand
the troubles my parents had. Especially my mother gave her best to not let me worry
about the obstacles she had to overcome.

Thanks to her I could focus on having fun and follow my passion. I started acting
in Frankfurt am Main in "theaterperipherie". We focused on pieces related to immigration. I also had the chance to make music and express myself in a creative way.

Now I want to give something back and currently I am using deep learning and machine learning in the Robert Koch Institute--Germany's public health institute--to utilize cutting edge technology in the health sector to improve the life of everybody.
